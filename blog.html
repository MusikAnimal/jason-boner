Welp, here goes nothing...

February 22, 2016

I love live entertainment. Whether it be a concert, a comedy show, a WWE pay-per-view, or a TV show taping, I live for being there to experience it. For close to 20 years now I have devoted much of my life (and my income) to supporting this obsession. Living in a city like New York makes it easy to get carried away with ticket buying; it's only February and I already have tickets for over 25 events!

With my calendar of events shaping up to promise a pretty epic year, I thought it might be fun to start a blog where I can chronicle my experiences and share them with other fans of live music and entertainment. I will not attempt to be artsy and prolific in my reviews
